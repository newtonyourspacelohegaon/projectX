# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

Welcome to **Vyb** (also referred to as "CampusConnect"), a social networking and dating platform designed for college students. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information generated through your use of the app:
- **Personal Profile Information:** Phone number (for authentication), full name, age, gender, username, and bio.
- **Campus Information:** College name, major, and graduation year to ensure a campus-specific experience.
- **Dating Profile Data:** If you use Dating Mode, we collect additional details such as height, hometown, dating intentions, and interests.
- **Media:** Photos and videos you upload to your profile, posts, or stories.
- **Social Interaction Data:** Information about your followers, following list, chats, and interactions with other users.
- **Technical Data:** Device information, IP address, and app usage statistics collected via our service providers (e.g., Expo/EAS).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Vyb experience.
- Verify your student status and connect you with your campus community.
- Facilitate social interactions, messaging, and dating matches.
- Personalize content and recommendations (e.g., interests and events).
- Ensure safety and security by monitoring for policy violations.
- Generate anonymous, aggregate statistics for internal research.

## 3. Permissions
To provide a full experience, Vyb requests the following permissions:
- **Camera:** To allow you to take and post photos/videos.
- **Microphone:** To record audio for posts or stories.
- **Photo Library:** To upload existing images for your profile or posts.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following cases:
- **With Other Users:** Your public profile information, posts, and dating profile details are visible to other users according to your settings.
- **Service Providers:** We use third-party vendors for hosting, media storage, and analytics who process data under our instructions.
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, we may disclose information to respond to legal requests or protect safety.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices
You have control over your data:
- **Edit Profile:** You can update your information within the app at any time.
- **Account Deletion:** You can request to delete your account through the app settings, which will remove your personal data from our active databases.
- **Blocking:** You can block users to prevent them from seeing your profile or interacting with you.

## 6. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Vyb is intended for college students and users over the age of 18 (or the legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
